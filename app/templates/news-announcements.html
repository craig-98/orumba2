📰 Announcements

Official announcements, public notices, and press releases from Orumba North Local Government.

January 15, 2025

2025 Budget Approved

The Executive Council has approved the 2025 budget of ₦2.5 billion, with 45% allocated to capital projects including roads, health centres, and school renovations across all 10 wards.

January 12, 2025

Free Medical Outreach Success

Over 5,000 residents received free healthcare services during our weekend outreach programme. The next outreach is scheduled for February across all wards.

January 10, 2025

Road Rehabilitation Commences

25km of strategic roads have been flagged off for rehabilitation. Phase 1 targets the Ihitenesa-Amaetiti and Nanka-Ufuma corridors.

January 8, 2025

Digital Skills Training Graduation

300 youths have successfully completed digital skills training. Certificates were awarded and startup funding announced for top performers.
