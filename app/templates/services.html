Our Services

Accessible, efficient, and transparent public services for every resident of Orumba North.

Street Naming Permit

Apply for official street naming and addressing permits to formalise your property and improve navigation within the LGA.

Birth Registration

Register births officially through our streamlined process. Obtain birth certificates that are recognised nationwide for school admission and identification.

Taxes & Levies

Information and processing for various chargeable taxes and levies. Pay your dues conveniently and support local development projects across all wards.

Marriage Registration

Formalise your union with an official marriage certificate issued by the LGA. Our registry staff ensure a smooth and dignified process for all couples.
