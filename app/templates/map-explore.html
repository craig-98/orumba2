Key Locations

LGA Secretariat

Ihitenesa — Administrative headquarters for all council operations and public enquiries.

General Hospital

Primary healthcare centre providing emergency, maternal, and outpatient services to residents.

Secondary Schools

Public and mission schools distributed across Agulu, Nanka, Nibo, and surrounding communities.

Major Markets

Ihitenesa and Okpeze markets serve as economic hubs for trade, agriculture, and commerce.

Agricultural Zones

Communal farmlands and cooperative societies supporting yam, cassava, and vegetable production.

Tourism Sites

Agulu Lake and cultural centres attracting visitors and researchers from across Nigeria.
