Orumba North History

From ancient Nri Kingdom to modern prosperity

1000 AD

Pre-Colonial Era

1000 AD - 1900. Nri Kingdom heartland with Igbo Ukwu bronze technology nearby.

1900

Colonial Period

1900-1960. British warrant chiefs. CMS missions establish Agulu Lake pilgrimage tradition.

1967

Biafran War

1967-1970. Biafra food production heartland sustaining the war effort through communal farming.

1991

LGA Creation

1991. Babangida era. Ihitenesa headquarters. 10 wards including Agulu, Nanka, Nibo.

2000

Modern Development

2000-Present. Infrastructure boom. NAU campus. Agulu Lake tourism. Population 200K+.
